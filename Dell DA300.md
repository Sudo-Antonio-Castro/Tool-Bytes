## What is it?
The **Dell DA300** is a compact USB-C 6 in 1 hub.

## What can you do?
Connect to an external display or projector
Get wired internet
Plug in USB devices

## Ports
**HDMI**  
**DisplayPort**  
**VGA**  
**RJ-45**  
**USB-A**
**USB-C**


### Tony's Use

This has saved me multiple times when I did not have a USB C to Ethernet Adapter, having multiple screens, and extra USB ports. 
It is a nice device to carry around, especially for how compact it is.
