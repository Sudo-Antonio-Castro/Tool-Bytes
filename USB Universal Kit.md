## What is it?
The **Philips USB 2.0 Universal Cable Kit** allows you to have multiple attachments **USB-B/Mini-B,Micro, and USB-C**.

## How to:
You plug the **USB-A** end into a USB-A port and pair the other end with one of the adapters.  
Thes adapters allow for charging and/or data.

### Information
**Speed**: USB 2.0
**Cable Length**: 6 Feet

#### Notes
I've used this product for various issues. Needing to test a printer, charging old devices, new devices, retrieving photos from cameras.  
I've also used just the extender cable for better reach.
